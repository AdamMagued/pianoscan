Engines
How PianoScan works.
🎯

Precise Logic Engine

A classical rule-based system that traces staff lines, note heads, and symbols with high precision. Works best on clean PDFs and sharp prints. Fastest option at 30–60 seconds.

🧬

Transformer AI

Uses HOMR, a TrOMR neural network purpose-built for polyphonic piano scores. Best accuracy on dense, complex pieces. Takes 2–3 minutes.

🤖

Deep Learning

GPU-accelerated deep learning model trained on thousands of scores. Handles imperfect images well — good for phone photos and slightly blurry scans. Takes 2–3 minutes.

TIP — Start with Precise Logic for PDFs and clean prints. Use Transformer AI for complex piano pieces where every note matters. Switch to Deep Learning for phone photos or handwritten scores.
